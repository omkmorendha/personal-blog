Every site needs a dummy post to kick things off. This is mine.

## Why write?

A few years into this career, I've noticed something: almost every great engineer I've worked with is also a great writer. I don't think that's a coincidence. Writing well and thinking well are basically the same muscle. You just flex it in different rooms.

So that's the whole point of this site. To make me think harder.

## What I'll write about

Mostly software. Distributed systems, tools I love (and tools I don't), architecture choices and why I made them. Sometimes the stuff around the edges too: how I learn, how I get work done, books that rearranged something in my head.
