Every site needs a first post. This is mine.

## Why write?

Writing forces you to find the edges of what you actually understand. When an idea lives only in your head, it's always in its best possible shape, fuzzy but promising. The moment you commit it to prose, you discover the gaps.

I've been an engineer for several years now and I've noticed a pattern: the best engineers I've worked with are almost universally good writers. Not because writing is required for the job, but because clear writing and clear thinking are the same skill practiced in different registers.

So this site exists for one reason: to make me think more carefully.

## What I'll write about

Mostly software, distributed systems, tools, architecture decisions, and the reasoning behind them. Occasionally things adjacent to that: how I learn, how I structure work, books that changed how I think.

I won't commit to a schedule. Posts will arrive when there's something worth saying.
